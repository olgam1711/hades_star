Blast Shield

Active shield. Protects the owner ship and all friendly ships within range against Area damage only. Direct weapon damage is not absorbed by this shield and is applied directly to the hull of the ship. Has priority over other shields when damage is applied to the area. Blueprints are available on RS6+
